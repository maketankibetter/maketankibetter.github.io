Wallpapers

Background change at the game and battle loader

Don't you like the background image at battle loading screen? Do you want turn back that background, that was at an event? This interfaces add-on will do that and you also will be able to put your favourite wallpapers with Hello Kitty!

FAQ

Q: Which images can be used?

A: You can use absolutely any image. If the image is located at tankionline.com domain (e.g. https://tankionline.com/en/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/r.jpg), you can use direct link, else you have to use specially developed service to generate specific link to the image.

Q: After pasting the specific link, my browser crashes. What should I do?

A: In this case you have to press the pen near to style and paste the specific image link between single quotes (you do not need to remove any quotes).
